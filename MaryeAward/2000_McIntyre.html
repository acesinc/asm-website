ASM's 2000 William B. Marye Award

Bill McIntyre


Bill McIntyre, an Archeological Society of Maryland member since 1980, has received the ASM's William B. Marye Award for 2000. Mr. McIntyre has served on many ASM committess, and has participated in all ASM field schools and on many surveys since 1992. Presently, he and Dan Coates are jointly heading the Garrett Island Archeological Survey in Cecil County.
